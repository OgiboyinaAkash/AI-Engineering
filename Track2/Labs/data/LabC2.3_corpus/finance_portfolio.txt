Portfolio rebalancing is the process of realigning the weights of assets to maintain a desired risk level. When a single sector such as technology exceeds 40 percent of total holdings, the portfolio becomes concentrated and vulnerable to sector-specific downturns. An AI agent can automate rebalancing by querying current holdings, identifying overweight sectors, ranking liquid assets for partial sale, requesting human approval before executing any trade, and logging every decision for audit purposes. Agents handling financial transactions must implement loop limits to prevent runaway order placement.
