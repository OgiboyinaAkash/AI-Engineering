Healthcare scheduling agents must operate under strict data privacy constraints. An agent booking appointments may read available time slots and doctor schedules but must never write to a patient record without explicit confirmation. High-stakes actions such as cancelling an existing appointment or booking a specialist referral require a human-in-the-loop approval step. All scheduling decisions must be logged with timestamps and the agent reasoning for audit and compliance purposes. Agents in regulated industries like healthcare must be designed so that every automated action is explainable and reversible where possible.
