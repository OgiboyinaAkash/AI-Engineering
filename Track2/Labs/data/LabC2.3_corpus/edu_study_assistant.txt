An AI study assistant acts as a bounded research agent for students. It searches approved academic sources, summarises findings in plain language, and saves notes to the student workspace. Safety boundaries prevent the assistant from accessing personal data outside the study session. The agent should explain its reasoning at each step so the student understands what was found and why it was selected. A good study agent turns each interaction into a learning moment rather than just a lookup service. Spaced repetition and retrieval practice are evidence-based techniques the agent can incorporate into a personalised study plan.
